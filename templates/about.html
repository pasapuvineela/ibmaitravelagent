The project

AI with a
practical purpose.

Roamly is an IBM SkillsBuild and AICTE internship project demonstrating how foundation models can solve a familiar, meaningful problem.

It combines a responsive Bootstrap interface, a beginner-friendly Flask backend, Netlify Functions, and IBM watsonx.ai. Granite or Llama models translate traveler preferences into day-wise schedules, recommendations, budget guidance, packing lists, and safety notes.
